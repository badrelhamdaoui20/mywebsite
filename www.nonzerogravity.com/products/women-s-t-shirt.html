30-Day Return & Exchange Policy

We stand behind our products and want our customers to be satisfied with their purchases. We accept returns for a full refund up to 30 days from the date of your order. Returned products must be in unused, sellable condition with all original labels, packaging, pamphlets, and inserts (if applicable). Returns Within The Contiguous U.S.

Shipping costs to return an item are not included. We can provide a prepaid shipping label for a flat fee, which will not be deducted from your refund amount. Please contact us directly with your order and item numbers to request a prepaid shipping label (applicable to the contiguous U.S. only).
